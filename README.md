‚úÖ Key insights

YYC (Calgary) emerges as the most globally connected secondary airport, with diverse routes to Europe, Asia, and the U.S.

YEG (Edmonton) primarily serves North American destinations, while YHZ (Halifax) and YOW (Ottawa) provide seasonal and business links to Europe and the Caribbean.

Smaller secondary airports have limited international reach, reflecting regional population and demand dynamics.

Airline strategies, geography, and regional economies play critical roles in determining which airports expand globally.
